Hi I am Vasil Tchanishvili
Evergreen student

I have a genuine passion for design, encompassing both 3D and 2D realms, and recently, I've added photography to my creative repertoire. Currently, my primary focus revolves around the captivating realm of 3D web development. I find joy in crafting immersive and visually striking digital experiences that come to life on the web. Exploring the intersection of creativity and technology, I constantly delve into various tools and techniques to elevate my designs and bring forth engaging interactive elements online. Alongside my digital endeavors, I also explore the world through the lens of photography, capturing moments and expressing my creativity in yet another visual medium.

Currently, I am employed as an HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) designer engineer, where my responsibilities involve creating efficient and effective HVAC systems for various projects. In this role, I contribute to designing systems that ensure optimal indoor air quality and climate control in diverse settings. Simultaneously, I am passionately engaged in expanding my skill set by delving into the realm of front-end web development. I find great satisfaction in learning the intricacies of web technologies, honing my abilities to craft visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces. This dual pursuit allows me to seamlessly blend my engineering expertise with creative endeavors in the digital space, enhancing both my technical and design capabilities.
